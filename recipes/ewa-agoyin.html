Ewa Agoyin and Fried Plantain

A plate containing boiled beans and sauce (popularly called Ewa Agoyin), paired with finely fried plantains.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups honey beans
  • 6 cups water
  • 1/2 - 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup shombo (reduce quantity if you don't like it spicy).
  • 3 large tatashe/bell peppers
  • 2 onions
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 maggi crayfish
  • 1 bouillon cube
  • 1/4 cup crayfish
  • plantain
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. To an empty pot, add your rinsed beans, water and some salt. Leave to boil for about 30 - 40 minutes.
